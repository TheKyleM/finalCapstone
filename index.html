Our Ranking

Coming first in our ranking is the elusive jordan XX8. Although it has a price point of $250, it is without the doubt one of the most elite shoes in the game right now. Second are the Kobe 9, this sheo introduced the extreme high tops mixed with the beloved Kobe silhouette combines for a deadly combo. Coming in third is the Kyrie 2, copying its predecessor the Kyrie 1, it is a shoe for quick athletes who need strong court grip. On the fourth spot we have the LeBron 14, a shoe truly fit for a king however it falls behind on the specs compared to the rest. finally we have the Curry 10, a good shoe by all means however it pales to it's predecessors in terms of style.
